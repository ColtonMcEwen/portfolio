Burger Builder

Web Application

Burger Builder is a personal project that was created to challenge my knowledge in JavaScript and React. It goes through a pretty straightforward process of building a burger with however many ingredients a person may want on it. Then the created burger may be ordered with a form to enter in contact data for a "delivery driver" to deliver the order.

Screenshot

On top this, there is signup/signin authentication for customers to keep track of all their burger orders and associated prices. The user and burger order data is secured and accessed through Google Firebase.
