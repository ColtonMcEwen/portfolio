Who I Am

Full-stack developer with a love of technology

I originally grew up in Northern Utah with a huge heart for technology. I did everything I could to get my hands on a computer. I practically became tech support to my grandparents! Did they pay me? Yes! Grandma's cooking.

Even though I enjoyed tinkering with technology, I had bigger ambitions to take my skills further...but with no idea how to get there. So I decided to go to college to take all sorts of computer classes. Design, web, database systems, programming, you name it and I've probably tried it. Did college help me narrow down what I want to do for a career? Not really. If anything, I wanted to do all of it.

Since graduating from Dixie State University in Southern Utah, I've realized that I can integrate all the skills I've learned into practically everything I do in some form or another. Every product or service needs to look good in design, connect to the web (obviously), data to store in servers, and most importantly, bringing it to life through code.

To that I say, why NOT do it all?
