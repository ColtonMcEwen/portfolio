Data Generation

Internship Project

Internships are not what I originally expected. I had high expectations of myself to develop code that could do amazing things without much effort. Boy was I wrong. I thought I knew how to code until I had this internship. On day one I had deer in the headlights with my assigned tasks.

Looking back, I realized that my assigned tasks took me weeks to accomplish compared to about a day or two with a seasoned developer. Needless to say, I learned humility in programming.

This internship partly consisted of writing two dozen Python scripts that generated over 1 million files into several data sets containing scraped HTML content with linked lists 5 levels deep for web crawling performance testing.

On top of this, I needed to document everything, step-by-step with straightforward instructions simple enough for a novice developer like me to understand.
